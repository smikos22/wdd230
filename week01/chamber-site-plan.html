Litchfield Park Chamber of Commerce

Purpose

The Litchfield Park Chamber of Commerce is a place to showcase what makes our town stand out. Business owners can promote services and gain feedback from local community members. New people moving into the area can look at this site and get a feel for everything this town has to offer.

Scenarios

What are the local events in the area? Where can I find different resturants? What school is my home zoned for?
